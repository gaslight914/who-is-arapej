Who is CYFI?

@arapej.1
CYFI, known professionally online as @arapej.1, is a rising digital creator and video editor hailing from Indonesia. At just 15 years old, he has carved out a unique space for himself in the mobile editing community. Embarking on his creative journey in 2023, CYFI has rapidly refined his skills, mastering complex visual styles that include high-energy Typography, complex Motion Graphics (Mograph), perfectly synced Velocity transitions, and cinematic Anime Music Videos (AMV / 3D). Driven by a passion for melancholic beats and intense visual storytelling, he continues to push the boundaries of mobile-based editing, proving that true artistry depends on vision and dedication rather than high-end gear.
